## Welcome to my personal website! ![Saudi Coffee Pot and Cup](images/coffee-pot-64.png)


I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science in the Department of Computer Science at Taibah University. I completed my PhD at RMIT University in 2025, where I worked with Professor [Mark Sanderson](http://marksanderson.org/), Professor [Falk Scholer](https://sites.google.com/view/fscholer/) (RMIT), and Dr [Paul Thomas](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/pathom/) (Microsoft). My thesis explored ways to *realise the ideal test collection*: [https://doi.org/10.25439/rmt.30633188](https://doi.org/10.25439/rmt.30633188).

My research lies in Information Retrieval (IR), focusing on evaluation, user variability, and the role of Large Language Models (LLMs) in developing IR evaluation resources. I am also committed to advancing Arabic IR through the development of evaluation resources and solutions grounded in Arabic and regionally relevant information needs, as well as fostering a sustainable research community. Through this work, I aim to promote more inclusive and language-diverse IR research.
